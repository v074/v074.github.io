I aim to obtain a job where I can put these skills to use, refining them in the process.

Education and Work History

I have two degrees, an Associate's of Arts in Pastry from Cincinnati State Technical and Community College, and a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science (with a double minor in Math and Psychology) from Northern Kentucky University, the latter with a Magna Cum Laude graduation. My time in computer science has introduced me to several languages, including Java, C, C++, and SQL, whereas my time in Pastry, while seemingly unconnected on the surface, parallels programming to a notable degree with both necessitating producing under a strict deadline and following strict procedures to get precise results.


My work experience has largely covered the food and customer service industry, having worked for two years in the on-site bakery at Cincinnati State, and one year at Holtman's Donuts. These jobs have been particularly enlightening to me, giving me an understanding of customer relations with companies as well as how to coordinate with a team under strict time constraints and sometimes unpredictable workloads.
